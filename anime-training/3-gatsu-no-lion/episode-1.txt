「Notes」
だった　é como se fosse でした na forma mais informal.
(だ＝です)

おらん is actually おる which comes from いる in standard japanese. It's a dialect thing. So in a connective form of a verb, you can turn the ておらん into a とらん by combining おらん with て. That is what happens in one of Rei's dad's dialogue lines.
